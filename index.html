Chapter Three

About & Travel

For ten days every July, Calgary becomes the centre of the cowboy universe. Since 1912, the Stampede has gathered the world’s best riders, the loudest cheers, and a city that wears its hat with pride.

Rodeo

The world’s richest outdoor rodeo. Bull riding, bronc, barrel racing.

Chuckwagons

Heart-stopping Rangeland Derby races every night.

Music

Stages across the park, from country anthems to headline pop.

Heritage

Indigenous powwow, Western art, and the Midway after dark.
